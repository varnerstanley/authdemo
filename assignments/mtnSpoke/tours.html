ADVENTURE TOURS

Jackson Creek

Need a good starter tour? Jackson Creek is just that. This 5 mile loop starts at our downtown location and heads east for a very scenic tour of Jackson Creek and Bozeman mountains. The tour includes 3 stops and the option to visit as many as 3 pizza establishments. More carbs the better for this brisk ride around town. Nothing beats filling your belly while basking in in the Bozeman atmosphere. Skill level is beginner to advanced. Each adult costs $50, and children $15.

Bridger Canyon

Can you last for 4 hours? How tough are you? This ride will test your stamina. This 5.7-mile ride up Bridger Canyon will test your limits in a gentle but firm way. We start downtown at our wonderful location and make our way east into Bridger Canyon. This tour gives you a scenic view of our Bozeman Mountains and the glory which is beheld within them. Pines, rivers, deer, moose, elk, or even your local beaver are some of the delights you can expect to see on this tour. There is 5 stops for a quick rest, a stop for lunch (included with tour) and a classy summer breeze just awaiting your arrival. Adults $55, Children $15.
